User's Benefits

Business Applications

Today, like never before javascript and node are shaping the way people write web applications. We have seen the massive response to the Ghost Kickstarter project, and we have decided to jump into the future and start extending Ghost right away. We have written an extension to Ghost that allows AngularJS components to run alongside your blog. Our vision, to create a marketplace of high quality components you can browse within your Ghost installation and install with a click. Each application will provide detailed information including test coverage, features, dependencies and comments to ensure a high level of quality. Ghost already has plugins on the roadmap, and we plan on fully incorporating our AngularJS components within their architecture when it is available. Imagine a wealth of components which include real time data from APIs all over the world. Then add the benefits of AngularJS for the developers; providing a rich api, high quality test suites, and an unbelivable community. Now you have a next generation blogging platform that is light years ahead of the standard WordPress sites you see today.
